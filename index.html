Built for NYC renter pain

Know what to verify before the listing wastes your time.

Rental AI does not guarantee legitimacy or provide legal advice. It does help you slow down and spot the questions that matter before touring or applying.

What we help check

  • Broker/no-fee claims, application fees, move-in totals, and utility or pet costs.
  • Payment-before-tour pressure, unsafe payment channels, missing address, and copied-looking marketplace text.
  • Commute tradeoffs, neighborhood fit, laundry/pet/internet needs, and manager/contact consistency.
  • Missing questions to ask the broker, landlord, or platform before you book a tour.
